Want to discover a new planet? Develop the cure for an infectious disease? Invent a new mode of transportation? Create an app that changes millions of lives around the world?

Hartshorn GEMS is an after-school club for girls in grades 3-5 who are fascinated by STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) or would simply love to discover their interests. Led by students from Millburn High School, it will feature investigative, hands-on projects introducing students to concepts in biology, chemistry, physics, engineering, math, and computer science. Meetings will be held weekly on Thursdays from 3 to 4 pm at Hartshorn, running parallel to the ASE schedule.
